About us

188 SPOWER CO.,LTD is a professional construction company operating on major projects throughout Vietnam (mostly in the north of Vietnam) for its many international and Vietnamese customers. We are also expanding its business interest into renewable energy project development, design and trading.

Management system Ⅰ

188 SPOWER CO.,LTD is committed to operating an effective and comprehensive Management System within its organization, reaching international standards.

Management system Ⅱ

188 SPOWER CO.,LTD management systems are applied from the time of invitation to tender/or negotiation with clients, through the contract process, construction and up to the end of the maintenance period.

Management system Ⅲ

188 SPOWER CO.,LTD management systems are continually reviewed, improved and adapted to individual project with the aim of guaranteeing the best result for each customer.

Traning program

Our internal training programmer ensure that all staff have a through knowledge and understanding of 188 SPOWER management system which enables them to implement and maintain this system within each and every project.

Customer satisfactions

Customer satisfactions are assessed at the completion of each project. We encourage our customers to submit an objective letter with their assessment of 188 SPOWER CO.,LTD performance throughout the project.

Safety and
Environment .

188 SPOWER CO.,LTD is a professional contractor in the construction industrial operating on sites in Vietnam.

It is the policy of 188 SPOWER CO.,LTD that any work must be carried out in a safe and eﬃcient manner at all work places. Occupational health and safety is regarded as a prerequisite in all company operations.

Human resources within the 188 SPOWER CO.,LTD organization is recognized as our most valuable asset. We regard safety as our 1st priority and consider that it is the responsible of 188 SPOWER CO.,LTD to ensure that workplace conditions in no way jeopardize the health and safety of any of our employees.

Safety in the company’s operation is developed in a comprehensive manner by close attention to work methods, equipment and by motivation and training of all our employees. Safety at all our workplace is carried out by setting standards and working together with our clients and other contractors to implement acceptable work practices.
